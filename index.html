Alex Mission Statement

Alex represents acceptance, love, equality, and Xenacious. It's mission is to promote a safe and compassionate community and coordinate educational opportunities for LGTBTQIA+ youth families and allies. By partnering with our local communities, our goal is to create a society where everybody is treated equally and fairly and can live their lives as their true authentic selves.
